About Me

Hi! I’m Carlos Daniel Aguilar Dominguez, currently pursuing a degree in Computer Technologies Engineering at Tecnológico de Monterrey. I’m driven by a passion for hands-on server work, network architecture, and systems administration.

My technical toolkit spans Kotlin (where I build and experiment daily) and core networking protocols (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP). I’ve also developed full-stack solutions—React front-ends talking to Node.js/Express back-ends—deployed on my own on-premises hardware.

Lately I’ve been deepening my skills in network automation (Ansible, shell scripting) and exploring IoT edge-computing scenarios—everything that prepares me for a role as a Network & Systems Administrator in a data-center or enterprise environment.

I’m eager to take on new challenges: designing resilient networks, optimizing server performance, and ensuring 24/7 uptime. If you’re looking for someone who loves to get hands-on with hardware, master protocols, and keep systems humming, let’s connect!
